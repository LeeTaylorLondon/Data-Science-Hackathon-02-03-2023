Innovation & Scalability

Traditional preventive maintenance tactics include routine inspections and scheduled repairs. The asset owners and maintenance professionals want to move from corrective and preventive maintenance to the condition based and predictive maintenance tactics. This will reduce maintenance costs and it will crease asset availability. Through the use of sensors and IoT data collection smart devices mounted on equipment, maintenance professionals will be able to monitor assets more effectively. Data collected from equipment will be stored in our IoT cloud application and connected to modern maintenance management systems, making it even easier to track important information including usage meters (products produced, mileage for vehicles) or condition monitoring such as temperature or vibration spikes. This information can then trigger work requests based on defined thresholds in the Maintenance Management platform. This feedback loop can happen in real time, improving reliability and productivity The solution is a SaaS solution so it is scalable and expandable in various verticals
